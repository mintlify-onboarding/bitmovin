## Support Matrixes

Bitmovin encodes MPEG-DASH, Apple HLS, Smooth Streaming, progressive TS, and progressive MP4 streams. With these formats you can reach all major devices. The streams can be played back with all major multimedia players and with the Bitmovin Player that provides you maximum device reach.

As seen below, Bitmovin supports a multitude of formats. To navigate the complexity of supported formats, and their combinations, 3 different support matrixes are provided and updated regularly:

* [Product Support Matrix - ENCODING - VOD Muxings](https://bitmovin.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/Product-Support-Matrix-ENCODING-VOD-Muxings.pdf)
* [Product Support Matrix - ENCODING - Live Muxings](https://bitmovin.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/Product-Support-Matrix-ENCODING-Live-Muxings.pdf)
* [Product Support Matrix - ENCODING - Subtitles & Captions](https://bitmovin.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/Product-Support-Matrix-ENCODING-Subtitles%5FCaptions.pdf)

_Disclaimer: Due to the complexity of combinations one can use with the Bitmovin Encoding product, those matrixes are for **guidance only** and can't be seen as promise or fact. Nor can they serve as basis for any claims. In doubt, please contact Bitmovin support via the support form in your dashboard._
