---
title: "How can I sign up through AWS Marketplace?"
---

When subscribing to the Bitmovin Video Encoding Services through the [AWS Marketplace](https://bitmovin.com/bitmovin-video-encoding-in-aws-marketplace/), a new Bitmovin Account is registered using the AWS Account Email Address. For this step to be successful, the following requirements do have to met:

* The email address of your AWS account is not yet associated with an Bitmovin account at [https://bitmovin.com/dashboard](https://bitmovin.com/dashboard)
